Our story

Preserving the Authentic Taste of Crete

NTOPION was founded with a singular purpose — to preserve and share the authentic spirit of Crete. From the sun-drenched vineyards to the mountain herbs, our homeland offers a rich palette of flavors that we proudly capture in every bottle.

Our journey began with a deep respect for tradition. Each bottle of raki, tsipouro, and liqueur is a tribute to generations of distillers who understood the balance between nature and craft. We use locally sourced grapes, herbs, and fruits, ensuring that every product reflects the true essence of the island.

Our Values

  • Tradition: We honor centuries-old recipes and methods.
  • Quality: Only the finest local ingredients go into our spirits.
  • Community: We support local farmers, craftsmen, and our Cretan heritage.
  • Sustainability: From vine to bottle, we practice mindful production that respects nature.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to connect people with the soul of Crete through taste. Whether you are sipping our tsipouro at a family gathering or gifting our liqueurs to a friend abroad, you’re sharing a piece of our island's warmth and authenticity.
